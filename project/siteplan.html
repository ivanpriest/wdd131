Site Plan

Site Name:Priest Dev Studios

This name represents a remote web and software development studios

Purpose

This site showcases our expertise as a Software and Web Developer specializing in Python, C#, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. It features my projects, skills, and technical insights in Web and Software development, UI/UX design, and software engineering. Whether you're an employer, client, or fellow developer, explore my work and connect for collaboration.

Scenarios:How this site helps you

1. Potential Client Looking for a Web & Software Developer

Sarah, a small business owner, needs a custom e-commerce website. She searches online for a web developer and finds this site. She explores the portfolio to see past projects, reads about expertise in Python, C#, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, and then contacts me to discuss her project.

2. Employer Looking to Hire

Mark, a hiring manager, is searching for a skilled developer. He visits the About and Resume sections to check experience. Impressed by the skills and projects, he downloads the resume and connects via LinkedIn or email.
